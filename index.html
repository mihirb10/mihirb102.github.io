Our Product

The new generation of social network: the all inclusive lifestyle management and building app

Stay Connected

We offer the fundamental social media tools of messaging, calls and news feeds. We bring social media to your finances, allowing you to share financial achievements and see how others are tackling their financial goals.

Settle Bills

Young people largely organise events and meetups through WhatsApp. We will help you take this one step further and automatically schedule events and split bills, eradicating the awkwardness that often follows.

Talk to your Heros

Join a squad with your favorite influencers, experts or celebrities chatting about the hot topics of the day. Seek personal advice from experts or tell the pundits what you think about the latest football transfer gossip.

Our Journey

How we got here and where we're headed.

  • October 2017

    The Incubator

    Padraic, Rose and Mihir quit their jobs and joined the Zinc startup incubator run by Localglobe. The incubator's mission was to create companies that improved mental and emotional health.

  • December 2017

    Forming a team

    From a cohort of 54, we found each other through our joint interests in making money fun, social and less taboo. We spent time speaking to young people to identify and understand exactly what the key pain points and problems to solve were.

  • January 2018

    Nailing down the idea

    We are currently in the prototyping & testing phase of our journey, refining the product until our users are happy with the experience it offers.

  • March 2018

    Build

    In March we hope to enter the build phase. At this stage we will be looking to expand our team and take on some preseed funding to help us do so. The Zinc incubator formally ends in March so from then on, we're on our own!
